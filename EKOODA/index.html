What is E-Waste?

E-waste refers to discarded electronic devices (e.g., phones, computers, TVs).These items contain hazardous materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium. Improper disposal can harm the environment and health.E-waste can be recycled to recover valuable materials (like gold, copper).Proper recycling reduces pollution and conserves resources.

Why Recycle E-Waste?

Protects the environment by preventing toxic chemicals from polluting land and water.Recovers valuable materials like gold and copper.Reduces pollution and supports a cleaner, sustainable planet.Helps conserve resources and reduce the need for mining.Improves health by avoiding exposure to hazardous waste.
